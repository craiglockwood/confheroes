Tips for speakers

Public speaking can be incredibly rewarding but taking that first step on to a stage can be terrifying.

We have all seen a talk where the speaker is lacking in confidence or is suffering from technical problems. More often than not, the difference between a great talk and an awkward shuffle through slides is tiny. Thankfully, anybody can learn to become a confident and engaging speaker.

Below are a bunch of resources that will help to equip you with the confidence to share your knowledge with others. Please feel free to add to this growing list of useful resources.
