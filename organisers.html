Tips for organisers

An event organisers work can be seen by some as a series of thankless tasks. Ironically, the events that appear to run smoothly and with the minimum of fuss are often the ones that have had the most amount of planning and preparation put into them.

Ask any event organiser and they will tell you that no two events are ever the same. This can make it incredibly hard to make the correct plans. The list of resources below will hopefully help future event organisers make smart decisions when it comes to running their own events.

Please feel free to add to this growing list of useful resources.


Submit a resource via GitHub

  • Conference organiser's handbook - Quirks Mode blog
  • How To Plan And Run A Great Conference Experience - Smashing Magazine
  • 20 Tips For Organising A Conference - Hi 2 Events
  • Your Event Planning and Management Advice Pack - Eventbrite blog
  • 10 Top Tips For Perfect Events - I Am Enterprises

You're going to pull your hair out over the tiniest of details when organising an event but don't ever give up. Nothing beats the thrill of seeing it all come to fruition and getting positive feedback from your attendees.
